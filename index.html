Features

Built for real life,
not spreadsheets.

💸
Log Expenses Instantly
Add any purchase in seconds — just enter the amount, pick a category, and you're done. No complicated setup required.
📊
See Where Money Goes
Beautiful charts show your spending by category and by month, so you always know the full picture at a glance.
🎯
Set Monthly Budgets
Create budgets for food, transport, fun — and get notified before you overspend. Stay in control effortlessly.
🕓
Full Transaction History
Browse all past expenses, search by name, or filter by category and date range to find exactly what you need.
☁️
Synced Across Devices
Your data is securely saved to Firebase — switch phones or reinstall the app and everything is still there.
📱
Works on iOS & Android
Built with Flutter for a smooth, native-feeling experience on any smartphone — iPhone or Android, no difference.
