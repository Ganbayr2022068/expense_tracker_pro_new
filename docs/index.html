Features

Everything you need
to own your finances.

💸
Log Expenses Instantly
Add any purchase in seconds. Just tap, enter the amount, pick a category, and you're done.
📊
Visual Analytics
See exactly where your money goes with beautiful charts broken down by category and month.
🎯
Budget Tracking
Set monthly budgets per category and get alerted before you overspend.
☁️
Cloud Sync via Firebase
Your data is securely synced — switch phones anytime and everything is still there.
🔐
Secure Login
Firebase Authentication keeps your account safe with email verification and password protection.
🌙
Dark Mode
Switch between light and dark themes to match your preference, day or night.
